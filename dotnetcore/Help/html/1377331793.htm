The CommandProcessor wants to support [CommandProcessor.Post{T}(T)] or Repost(Guid) using Task Queues. You need to provide a policy to specify how QoS issues, specifically [CommandProcessor.RETRYPOLICY ] or [CommandProcessor.CIRCUITBREAKER ] are handled by adding appropriate Policies(IAmAPolicyRegistry) when choosing this option.
